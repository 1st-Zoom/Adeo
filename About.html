Who We Are

We are a leading smart space and smart city solution provider. Our solutions are optimized to run under a variety of bandwidth conditions and networks - from regular LAN and WiFi to 4G/LTE and 5G; from stationary locations to moving vehicles and locomotives.

Our computer vision, AI and deep machine learning solutions can seamlessly integrated with both existing and new security cameras. In most cases, customers do not need to “rip and replace” their CCTV (analog or digital) setups. We are able to add groundbreaking intelligence layered on top of existing hardware investments.

Our solutions can be flexibly customized for enterprise and government customer needs of any scale – from dozens to thousands of cameras, from one to thousands of locations.
