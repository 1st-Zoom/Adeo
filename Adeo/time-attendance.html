Time and Attendance

Get Started with Chime™

Chime™ is cloud-based software that enables businesses to simplify scheduling staff and workers on various locations. Staff and workers can automatically check-in and check-out from their job appointments. Administrators can invite, schedule, track and obtain detailed report for workers. Chime™ saves time and money for organizations and digitally transforms their day to day office and field operations.
